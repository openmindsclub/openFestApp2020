OpenMindsClub Joins Hacktoberfest 2020

October 11-13th, 2020 • Online

or Check out the repo

About The Event

Event Begins In

Openfest is a 3-day online event, it will first start with a FOS Talk (+ Event Introduction) as well as a Git/Github Workshop on October 11th, for the beginners who want to learn more about Open Source and how to contribute to Open Source projects. The 12th is when we get back to business with the big Fest!

Participants will work with each other to contribute to our Hacktobefest Github repository in the hopes of ending up among the first 50,000 people to complete the challenge and winning DigitalOcean's awesome prizes!

Organizer

Philosophy

OpenMindsClub is a USTHB scientific club, our goal is to promote collaboration through the mutual sharing of knowledge and the philosophy of Free Open Source among the university students on a smaller scale... and nationally on a larger one.


Activities

"That Which Is Not Shared Is Lost," a fundamental ethic which we strive to honor through various events and activities among which is The Install Party, one of our main, dedicated to the gathering of the Open Source community for the traditional GNU/Linux installations, in addition to a collection of conferences, courses, workshops, contests and other activities about Programming, Electronics, Foreign Languages, Photography, Design... and much more, all of which are meticulously prepared by our members throughout the year.


They Trusted Us


Prizes

In addition to having lots of fun, you can earn a limited version t-shirt and ton of hacktoberfest swag (Stickers and more!) if you happen to be among the first 50,000 participants who successfully complete the challenge! This year in particular, OpenMinds Club is inviting you to participate to our Openfest for chances of winning extra symbolic prizes:

Certificate (Signed and stamped by the president of the OpenMindsClub).

OpenMindsClub Sticker.

Exclusive "Contributor" Sticker.

2 Guaranteed places for a future event, workshop or course of your choice.

Github Account Exposure​.

Challenges:

That is if you succeed to land the first prize in one of our set categories of challenges, namely:

Brave Benefactor: most number of Pull Requests made to the repo.

Story Sensation: highest change rate (additions+deletions) to the Open Source Story project.

Diligent Developer: most number of Pull Requests made to IT-themed projects.

Erudite Editor: best video montage in the Montage Contest.

Logo Looney: best OpenMindsClub's logo redesign in the Redesign Competition.

Schedule

The tentative program for OpenFest is as follows (all times are local i.e. GMT+1). The event will be a 3-day event.

10:00 — 11:00

FOS Talk and Event Introduction

Understand Hacktoberfest and how to participate and cease the opportunity to familiarize yourself with Open Source. A Q&A will be held at the end of the session where all inquiries will be answered by the speaker.
Sunday 11th,
14:00 — 16:00

Join the hands-on workshop to learn the basics of Git/Github, a powerful tool in the community, to share and collaborate on online projects.

Open Fest

A fest where participants work together in an atmosphere of collaboration, guided by our maintainers who will help them achieve the challenges and be eligible rank among the first 50,000 and win DigitalOcean's and Openmindsclub's special prizes.

Get Involved

Everyone is welcome to Hacktoberfest as the participants, so far, have represented 151 countries and thousands of unique skill-sets. Its program welcomes everyone already in the open source software community – and, as a matter of course, anyone interested in diving in!​

To qualify for the official challenge, you must register on DigitalOcean's official website and then make four pull requests between October 1-31. If you want to participate alongside OpenMindsClub, click on the register button on top and wait for our confirmation e-mail to come during the upcoming days!

Of course! We have created many projects that you can contribute to whether you are a programmer or not! But if you want to learn more about Open Source and how to participate, we recommend you join the us on the first day of the event and attend both FOS Talk and the Git/Github Workshop.
